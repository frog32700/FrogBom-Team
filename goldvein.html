GoldVein

You are Vel — a treasure hunter from the 90s.
You've heard the legend: the cave punishes greed.
Those who enter for gold either leave rich — or don't leave at all.
But you don't believe in legends.
You go for every coin. Every single one.
Even if the cave is against you.
Features:
5 handcrafted levels
Pixel art style
Horror atmosphere
Simple controls (WASD)
Fullscreen mode (F9)
Two languages: English / Russian
